Best Time to Visit Kalapathar Beach


The finest time to enjoy Kalapathar Beach is in the spring, and from October to May, it gives the most amazing experiences. Make plans to visit this location throughout the months above if you wish to catch its stunning charm. Though there would undoubtedly be fewer people than at any other Andaman beach, given that these are the busiest travel months, you might find the beach to be a little congested.


How to Reach Kalapathar Beach
Via Air Through Water Bypass Road

The best method to get here if you're traveling to the Andaman Islands is via air. You can take a taxi or cab from the Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair to Havelock Island. The airport is well-connected to all of India's major cities. Though it is the priciest way to go to Kalapathar Beach, taking a flight is unquestionably the finest choice if you want luxury and pleasant travel.

Additionally, you can take any ferry that runs to Port Blair from Chennai or Kolkata. Even though the trip would take a while, you would be able to see its breathtaking scenery. You can take a direct taxi or cab to Kalapathar Island once you arrive in Port Blair.

From Rangat, Baratang Island, and Diglipur, you can take any of the several public or private buses that head to Kalapathar Beach. Even though it is the least expensive way to go to the beach, buses are only available from a few specific places.
