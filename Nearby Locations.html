Places To Visit Near Kalapathar Beach

Neil Island

A little island called Neil Island is south of the Andaman Sea. You will fall in love with this island because of its less-explored marine diversity, beaches, coral reefs, and alluringly lush vegetation. The uncovered adventures to Neil Island will undoubtedly surprise you, whether you're searching for an exciting water sports activity or an unforgettable getaway with your partner!
What to Do: Glass bottom rides and snorkeling
Distance From Port Blair: 36 km

Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach is a breathtaking beach that is close to Kalapathar Beach. Radhanagar Beach, which is ranked among the top 10 beaches in the world, has been drawing tourists for generations with its dazzling splendor. The closest beach to Havelock Island, sometimes referred to locally as Beach No. 7, provides the greatest beach experiences.
What to Do: Swimming, Scuba Diving, Snorkeling, and Parasailing
Distance From Port Blair: 72 km

Elephant Beach

Elephant Beach is among the most fascinating locations to explore close to Kalapathar Beach. Elephant Beach offers breathtaking undersea marine life and colorful coral reefs—everything you need for the ideal family getaway! It's a fantastic vacation destination that's highly known for its coral reefs, which draw tourists from all over the world.
What to Do:Mirror boating rides, diving, snorkeling, swimming, Kayaking, and Trekking
Distance From Port Blair: 70 km
